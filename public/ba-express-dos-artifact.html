How the lifecycle loop works

Every active driver carries four rolling deadlines: DVLA recheck (annual), UK CRC refresh (every 3 years), RTW expiry (if visa-based), and training refresh (annual). The system fires emails at D-30, D-7 and D-0 to both the driver and the Director. If the driver doesn't action by D-0, the system places them on hold (cannot be assigned a route) until cleared.

This runs as a Cowork scheduled task — daily at 08:00 it checks every active driver, fires the relevant emails, and updates the dashboard alerts.
